Explore Breda
  • Research:

    Identified Breda’s tourist categories and typical visitors. Conducted interviews with locals and tourists to gather insights. Investigated tourist preferences, behaviors, and competitor strategies.

    Tourist Preferences in Breda:
    Enjoy cultural landmarks like Breda Castle and Grote Kerk, outdoor activities in Mastbos forest and on the Mark river, and sampling local and international cuisine, including festivals like the Breda Jazz Festival.

    Visitor Behavior:
    Tourists use online (websites, social media) and offline (visitor centers, local advice) resources to plan visits.

    Competitor Analysis:
    Compared to cities like Amsterdam, Rotterdam, The Hague, and Utrecht, Breda highlights its history, culture, and natural attractions through digital marketing and targeted events.
